Game Overview

In Rat Attack, you, the scientist, must fend off the swarm of rats that infest your lab! Using nothing but your only weapon, a flask of harmful chemicals, moving from 3 stages, your lab, and the cave where the Rat Boss lies. Shooting and aiming with the flasks using M1 damages the enemy rat’s health, but be careful, as you have health of your own. Taking too much damage results in death so be careful! Each stage consists of a number of rats. These rats are very dangerous and can kill you easily. At the last stage, you face Pete the alpha rat. He is extremely powerful and you must be very careful when facing him. You never know what tricks he may have up his rat sleeves.

Gameplay

Rat Attack has very simple and universal controls. Playing as the scientist there are two ways to traverse the map, WASD controls and arrow key controls.

-To move up: W Key/Up Arrow Key

-To move down: S Key/Down Arrow Key

-To move left: A Key/Left Arrow Key

-To move right: D Key/Right Arrow Key

-To attack : Mouse 1 (throws flask)

When attacking, the scientist throws a flask of harmful chemicals, dealing damage to the rats. You, as the scientist, must kill all the rats to proceed to the next stage.

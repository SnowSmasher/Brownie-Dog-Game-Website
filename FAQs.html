FAQs

How long did it take you to make the game?

2 Weeks

How did you make all the art?

First, our artist John drew the assets on paper. We then used a printer to scan the images and turn them into jpg files for our game. We then coloured the pictures with Adobe Photoshop and added them to our game with unity.

Are you planning to sell your game in the future?

No.

What game engine did you use?

As mentioned before, we used Unity to create our game.

Have any of you created a game before?

We have all actually created our own games before as part of IST.

Do you enjoy making games?

Yes, we all love making games. We also love playing games like Overwatch.

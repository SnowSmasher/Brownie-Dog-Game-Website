Artwork

The assets and artworks, aside from our logo, was completely hand drawn by our Graphics Designer John Pham. We aimed for a simple design for our game and tried to avoid making it too detailed. Focusing on the design and layering of the map, we believe, builds atmosphere and this lab setting. Although the design of the scientist is quite simple, the design on the enemy rats, especially the Rat Boss, is detailed.
